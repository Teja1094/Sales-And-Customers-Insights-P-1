Sales And Customers Insights 

1. Project Overview
   
This project focuses on building an interactive Business Performance Dashboard using Power BI to analyze key areas such as sales, profit, customers, returns, and shipping operations.
The dashboard serves as a centralized platform for decision-makers to monitor performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions. 
It integrates multiple data sources into a single, user-friendly interface with dynamic filtering and drill-down capabilities.

2. Objectives
   
•	Track overall business performance (Sales, Profit, Orders) 
•	Understand customer behavior and segmentation 
•	Analyze regional performance 
•	Monitor returns and shipping efficiency 
•	Identify trends and growth opportunities 

3. Tools Used
   
•	Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation 
•	DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Creating calculated measures and KPIs 
•	Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation 
•	Excel / CSV Dataset – Data source 

4. Description of Dashboard Pages
   
1. Executive Overview
   
Provides a high-level snapshot of business performance.
Includes KPI cards (Sales, Profit, Orders, Customers), trend lines, and YoY growth indicators for quick decision-making.

2. Sales & Profit Analysis

Focuses on revenue and profitability.
Highlights top-performing categories, sub-categories, and products, along with profit vs sales comparisons.

3. Geography Analysis

Displays regional performance using maps and charts.
Helps identify high-performing and underperforming regions.

4. Segment Analysis

Analyzes customer segments such as Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office.
Shows contribution to sales and profit along with behavioral patterns.

5. Returns Analysis

Tracks return rates and returned orders.
Identifies products or areas causing losses due to returns.

6. Shipping Analysis

Evaluates logistics performance.
Shows shipping modes, delivery trends, and cost efficiency.


5. Key Insights
   
•	Certain regions contribute the majority of revenue, indicating strong market presence 
•	A few product categories generate high sales but low profit margins 
•	Customer segments like Consumer drive the largest share of sales 
•	High return rates in specific products negatively impact profitability 
•	Shipping modes vary in cost-efficiency, indicating opportunities for optimization 
•	Seasonal trends influence sales performance significantly
